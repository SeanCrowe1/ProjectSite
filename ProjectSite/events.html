Dungeons & Dragons Tournament

28/02/2026

Our 3rd annual Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) Tournament will be held on the 28th of February, 2026, where multiple teams of players will run through the same pre-written series of challenges, and are scored based on their performance. The goal is to determine which team completes objectives most effectively within a time limit. If you think you are up to the challenge, come down before 12:00 noon on the day of the tournament and enter the battle.



Warhammer 40K Tournament

21/02/2026

We are delighted to announce thata Warhammer 40,000 tournament is going to be held in our store for the first time ever on the 21st of February, 2026. This will be a competitive event where players use their custom-built and painted miniature armies to play multiple rounds of the tabletop game in a structured format. The events combine strategic gaming skill with a celebration of the hobby's modelling and painting aspects. There will also be a mnifigure painting workshop on the 20th of February for any newcomers who would like some guidance on getting set up or veterans who just want to touch up their moddles before the war.
